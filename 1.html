Welcome

We warmly welcome you to AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit. Our society is dedicated to the vision of empowering rural communities through cooperation, trust, and self-reliance. Established with the belief that collective growth is the foundation of sustainable development, the society strives to bring financial stability, social welfare, and equal opportunities to every member of the community. The cooperative is not just an institution but a platform where individuals come together to support one another, to share resources, and to uplift the standard of living of rural families. Through our programs, services, and initiatives, we focus on improving financial literacy, creating avenues for employment, and ensuring that the benefits of cooperation reach the grassroots level. We firmly believe that the strength of our society lies in the active participation of our members. Each member plays an important role in shaping the future of this cooperative. By joining hands, we are not only addressing immediate financial needs but also laying the foundation for long-term prosperity and stability in rural areas.

    Our commitment is to foster:
  • Collective Growth: Working together to achieve shared goals.
  • Self-Reliance: Encouraging independence and empowerment among members.
  • Sustainability: Promoting practices that ensure long-term development.
  • Social Responsibility: Contributing to the welfare of the entire community.

As you become a part of this journey, we extend our gratitude for your trust and faith in the cooperative movement. With your support, AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit will continue to be a guiding light in strengthening communities, promoting equality, and building a brighter future for generations to come.

About Us

AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit is a cooperative institution established with the objective of promoting financial inclusion, social development, and economic empowerment in rural areas. Founded on the principles of cooperation, transparency, and mutual support, the society serves as a trusted platform where individuals unite to achieve common goals for collective prosperity. Our society works with the mission of improving the quality of life of its members by providing reliable financial services, encouraging savings, and offering support in times of need. We strongly believe that development is most effective when it comes from within the community, and hence, our cooperative model ensures active participation of every member in decision-making. Over the years, the society has built a reputation of integrity and service to its members. With dedicated leadership and enthusiastic participation, we have created opportunities for sustainable growth while fostering a culture of trust and cooperation.

    Key Features of Our Society:
  • Financial Empowerment: Offering safe and accessible financial solutions.
  • Community Development: Strengthening rural livelihoods through cooperation.
  • Transparency & Trust: Ensuring fairness in every process.
  • Member-Oriented Services: Prioritizing the needs and welfare of members.
  • Sustainability: Focusing on long-term benefits rather than short-term gains.

    • The society continues to stand as a symbol of unity and progress in the rural community. With every initiative, we remain committed to serving our members with honesty, dedication, and responsibility, ensuring that AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit remains a guiding institution for rural growth and cooperative strength.

History

The foundation of AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit is rooted in the vision and dedication of a single individual, Shri Kishor Pandurang Kawre. With a strong belief in the principles of cooperation and community development, he initiated the idea of forming a cooperative society to support the financial and social needs of people in rural areas. His determination and leadership led to the establishment of the society, which has now grown into a trusted institution serving numerous members. What began as the dream of one person has today transformed into a collective movement, bringing together individuals under one platform for mutual benefit and prosperity. Over the years, the society has expanded its activities, introduced new services, and strengthened its role as a pillar of financial support and community development. The efforts of Shri Kishor Pandurang Kawre continue to inspire members and guide the society towards greater heights.

Through our vision, we aim to transform collective strength into meaningful progress, making AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit a pillar of trust and growth for generations to come.

Membership

Any individual can become a member of AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit by depositing a certain amount of money as share capital or membership fee, as decided by the society. Membership is open to all who are willing to follow the rules, regulations, and principles of the cooperative society.

    Key Points of Membership:
  • A person becomes a member by depositing the required share amount with the society.
  • Every member enjoys equal rights and responsibilities irrespective of the amount of money contributed.
  • Members are entitled to avail financial and non-financial services offered by the society.
  • Every member has the right to participate in decision-making and elect representatives in the society‚Äôs management.
  • Membership promotes the spirit of unity, cooperation, and collective progress.

Thus, becoming a member is not only about depositing money but also about joining hands in building a strong and self-reliant cooperative institution.

Benefits Of Membership

    Key Benefits:
  • Low-Interest Loans: Members can avail loans at comparatively lower rates of interest than market or private lenders, making financial support easily accessible.
  • Encouragement of Savings: Safe deposit and recurring savings schemes are available for members to build financial discipline.
  • Profit Sharing: Surplus income or profit earned by the society is distributed among members in proportion to their participation.
  • Financial Security: Members get access to emergency financial support during times of need.
  • Democratic Participation: Every member has equal voting rights and can actively participate in decision-making.
  • Community Development: Benefits are not limited to individuals but extend to the entire rural community through cooperative efforts.
    • Thus, membership ensures not only economic relief through low-interest loans but also strengthens the spirit of cooperation, unity, and mutual growth.

The society focuses on providing easy access to loans as well as safe and reliable savings options.

Activities

The AAI Jagdamba Gramin Sahakari Pat Sanstha Maryadit is actively engaged in providing various banking and credit facilities to its members. The core objective of the society‚Äôs activities is to ensure financial inclusion, promote savings, and make credit accessible at reasonable terms.

    Banking Facilities
  • The society offers multiple deposit schemes such as Saving Accounts, Recurring Deposits (RD), Fixed Deposits (FD), and Daily Collection (DL), allowing members to save systematically according to their convenience.
  • These banking services not only encourage the habit of saving among rural households but also provide them with a secure place to keep their earnings.
  • The society also ensures transparency and trust in its banking operations, creating a reliable financial environment for its members.
    Credit Facilities
  • The society provides Regular Loans, Loans on FD, Loans on RD, and Daily Loans (DL), supporting members in times of financial need.
  • Credit facilities are extended at reasonable interest rates, making them highly beneficial as compared to traditional moneylenders.
  • These loans help members in meeting personal, agricultural, business, and emergency expenses without financial stress.
  • By offering such credit services, the society plays a vital role in promoting self-reliance and economic growth among its members.

Community-Oriented Activities Apart from financial services, the society also participates in activities that strengthen the sense of cooperation and community welfare. Awareness programs are organized to educate members about savings, loans, and financial discipline. Through these efforts, the society is not only fulfilling its role as a financial institution but also contributing to the overall development of rural society.
